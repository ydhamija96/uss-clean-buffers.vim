*uss-clean-buffers.txt*  UltaSuperSimple Function to clear hidden buffers

Author:  Yogesh Dhamija <ydhamija96.github.io>

                                                *:CloseHiddenBuffers*
:CloseHiddenBuffers[!]      Closes all hidden buffers that don't have unsaved changes. If ! is used,
                            also close hidden terminal buffers.

                                                *:CLO*
:CLO[!]                     |:CloseHiddenBuffers|
